Vodka Rigatoni Recipe

Ingredients

  • Heavy Cream
  • Crushed Tomatoes
  • Vodka
  • Red Peppers
  • Black Peppers
  • Parsley
  • White Sugar
  • Garlic Powder
  • Salt
  • Rigatoni Pasta
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Drain roasted peppers, reserving a small amount of the liquid. Cut peppers into strips.
  2. Combine cream, crushed tomatoes, vodka, roasted peppers with reserved liquid, parsley, garlic powder, sugar, salt and black pepper, and crushed red pepper in a saucepan. Cover. Cook over medium heat, stirring often, until the sauce comes to a boil. Reduce heat, and simmer for 30 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, cook pasta according to package directions. Drain, and transfer to a large serving bowl.
  4. Stir cheese into the sauce and stir sauce into rigatoni
